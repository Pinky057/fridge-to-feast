Built for Everyone

Fridge-to-Feast is designed with accessibility at its core. Our full voice mode enables visually impaired users to enjoy a complete cooking experience without ever needing to look at the screen.

  • Wake Word Activation - Just say "Hey Chef" to start
  • Full Voice Navigation - Control everything by voice
  • Audio Feedback - Chef speaks all instructions clearly
  • Interrupt Anytime - Natural conversation flow
  • No Visual Required - 100% screen-free cooking
